# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Metriflash ("the App") handles your information. The short version: it doesn't.

## Summary

Metriflash collects no data. It makes no network requests. It contains no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

Metriflash does **not** collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. This includes but is not limited to:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers or fingerprints
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports

## Network Access

Metriflash makes **zero** network requests. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any server, API, or external service.

## Local Storage

The app stores your settings and profiles (Guide Number, ISO, compensation values) locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

## Third-Party Services

Metriflash does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, libraries, or frameworks that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Because Metriflash collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. There is no data collection from children under 13 (or any other age group).

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Since the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
